## Daily Structure (100+ dials/day target)

### 08:00-10:00 — Call Block 1 (PRIME TIME)
- Power dialer through pre-scored list
- Target: 50-60 dials = 5-6 conversations = 2-3 demos

### 10:15-12:00 — Call Block 2
- Continue + retry block 1 no-answers
- Target: 40-50 dials = 4-5 conversations = 2-3 demos

### 12:30-16:00 — Demo Block
- 5 demo slots (30 min + 15 min buffer each)
- Fireflies records + AI summary → Pipedrive

### 16:00-17:00 — Afternoon Calls
- Callbacks + decision-makers wrapping up
- Target: 20-30 dials = 1-2 demos

### Daily Totals: 110-140 dials, 12-15 conversations, 6-8 demos booked

## Key Automations (Make.com)

1. **Lead Enrichment Pipeline:** Apollo → enrich → Pipedrive + SPIN notes
2. **Post-Call Follow-Up:** Call outcome → email sequence or demo booking
3. **Demo Booked Flow:** Cal.com → Pipedrive deal + prep email + calendar
4. **Post-Demo Follow-Up:** Fireflies AI summary → Pipedrive notes + email sequence
5. **No-Show Recovery:** Auto "sorry we missed you" + reschedule link

## Best Czech Calling Times
- **Peak days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Peak hours:** 9:00-11:00, 14:00-16:00
- **Avoid:** Monday 8-10am, Friday afternoon, 12:00-13:30

## Lead Scoring (ICP Matrix)

### Firmographic Fit (max 40 pts)
- 100-500 employees: +15
- 500-1000: +10
- Industry (manufacturing, IT, services, logistics): +10
- Prague/Brno/Ostrava: +5
- Has HR Director/Manager: +10

### Buying Signals (max 40 pts)
- Hiring 5+ roles: +15
- New HR Director (last 6 months): +15
- 20%+ headcount growth: +10
- Recently raised funding: +10

### Engagement (max 20 pts)
- Booked demo via website: +20
- Opened 3+ emails: +5
- Clicked link: +5
- Visited pricing page: +10
- No activity 14+ days: -10 (decay)

### Thresholds
- 80+ = HOT — call today
- 50-79 = WARM — call this week
- 30-49 = COOL — email sequence
- <30 = PARK

## Qualification: Use CHAMP (not BANT)

| Letter | Question |
|--------|----------|
| **C** Challenges | "How do you currently evaluate candidates beyond CV and interview?" |
| **H** Authority | "Who's involved in decisions about HR tools?" |
| **A** Money | "Companies your size typically invest X-Y CZK — roughly in line?" |
| **M** Priority | "Where does improving hiring quality sit on your priorities this quarter?" |

On cold calls, only need C + H to qualify for demo.
