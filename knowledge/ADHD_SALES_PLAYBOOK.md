## The Science

- ADHD brain has 40% less dopamine activity in motivation/reward areas
- 60% of entrepreneurs exhibit ADHD traits; 5x more likely to start a business
- Delayed rewards feel physically painful → need IMMEDIATE feedback loops
- Gamification creates artificial dopamine hits through streaks, points, visual progress

## Daily Structure (ADHD-Optimized Pomodoros)

| Time | Block | Activity | Dopamine Source | Duration |
|------|-------|----------|----------------|----------|
| 08:00-08:25 | Sprint 1 | Hot prospect calls (top 3 from lead score) | Conversation energy | 25 min |
| 08:30-08:55 | Sprint 2 | Personalized video messages (Loom, 60s each) | Creative expression | 25 min |
| 09:00-09:25 | Sprint 3 | LinkedIn content (1 post) | Audience engagement | 25 min |
| 09:30-09:55 | Sprint 4 | Follow-up emails (AI-drafted, review) | Clearing the queue | 25 min |
| 10:00+ | Deep Work | Discovery calls, demos, proposals | Hyperfocus zone | Flexible |

### Modified Pomodoro Rules for ADHD Sales
- 15/5 for prospecting (shorter = less initiation resistance)
- 45/15 for deep work (let hyperfocus run when it hits)
- NEVER mix task types within a block
- Visual timer (physical sand timer > digital)
- If you're in flow on a call — DON'T STOP. Skip the break.

## The 20% Rule
On discovery calls, talk MAX 20% of the time. Listen 80%.
This is HARD for ADHD because hyperfixation kicks in on topics you're passionate about.
Put a physical reminder in view: "POSLOUCHEJ" (LISTEN)

## Dopamine-First Task Design
1. Start EVERY day with the fun stuff — hot prospect call or creative outreach
2. Break large tasks into micro-tasks (3-5 minutes each)
3. Each tiny completion = dopamine hit
4. The Dopamine Bridge: pair every sales task with something enjoyable:
   - Prospecting + favorite playlist
   - Call prep + good coffee
   - CRM updates + standing desk
5. Novelty injection: rotate channels daily (email Monday, LinkedIn Tuesday, phone Wednesday)

## Body Doubling
- Book Focusmate session at 9 AM daily (virtual accountability partner)
- Tell your partner: "Dělám sales cally 50 minut"
- The social contract makes you actually do it
- Alternative: find a founder accountability partner (weekly metrics check-in)

## Morning Briefing ADHD Rules
- Max 3 priorities (more = decision paralysis)
- Show 5 hottest leads ONLY (not 50)
- Big money number at top (motivational)
- Streak counter visible
- Today's gamification target visible
- ONE sentence about yesterday's win (dopamine from memory)

## Signal-Based Selling (THE methodology of 2026)

Instead of spray-and-pray, trigger outreach based on BUYING SIGNALS:

| Signal | Detection | Priority | Outreach Template |
|--------|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| HR job posting | LinkedIn / careers page | 🔥 HOT | "Rostete — jak měříte engagement v nových týmech?" |
| Funding round | Crunchbase / news | 🔥 HOT | "Gratulace k [round]. Jak investujete do lidí?" |
| New CEO/CHRO | LinkedIn job change | 🟡 WARM | "Noví lídři často chtějí data — jak měříte engagement?" |
| Glassdoor complaints | Glassdoor monitoring | 🔥 HOT | "Zaznamenal jsem [specific issue]. Řešíte to?" |
| Growth milestone | LinkedIn headcount | 🟡 WARM | "Přes [X] lidí — neformální engagement se rozpadá" |
| Competitor evaluation | G2/Capterra | 🔥 HOT | "Porovnáváte engagement nástroje? Mám srovnání." |

Speed-to-signal is critical: top teams act within 30 minutes of signal detection.
