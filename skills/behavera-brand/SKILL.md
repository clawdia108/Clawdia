## Product: Echo Pulse
- AI-powered pulse surveys via chatbot "ECHO"
- Measures 12 key engagement factors:
  1. Vztah s nadřízeným (Manager relationship)
  2. Autonomie (Autonomy)
  3. Smysluplnost práce (Meaningful work)
  4. Kariérní růst (Career growth)
  5. Uznání (Recognition)
  6. Work-life balance
  7. Kompenzace a benefity (Compensation)
  8. Firemní kultura (Company culture)
  9. Komunikace (Communication)
  10. Psychologické bezpečí (Psychological safety)
  11. Týmová spolupráce (Team collaboration)
  12. Inovace a změna (Innovation & change)
- 3 minutes to fill, results in 24 hours
- Anonymous or non-anonymous
- Gamified chatbot format (not boring surveys)
- AI-generated recommendations — not just data, actionable insights
- Based on behavioral science + I/O psychology

## Brand Voice
- **Character:** "Chytry kolega v kavarne" — sharp, warm, data-driven
- **Formality:** Semi-formal Czech. Vykani first contact, relaxed inside.
- **Directness:** State benefits as facts, not hopes
- **Humor:** Light, occasional, never forced
- **Empathy:** Acknowledge their situation before any pitch

### Forbidden
- "V dnesnim dynamickem prostredi"
- "synergicke efekty", "holisticky pristup", "komplexni reseni"
- Superlatives without proof
- Pressure tactics
- American-style enthusiasm ("Amazing!", "Game-changer!")
- Generic corporate speak

### Signature Patterns
- "Navazuje to na..." (mirrors back prospect's words)
- Two-option close (A/B, different commitment levels)
- Short paragraphs (max 3-4 sentences)
- Czech + English terms: "tiche odchazeni (quiet quitting)"
- Data first, story second

## CEO Fears (understand before every call)
1. **"Results show I'm a bad leader"** → Data shows TEAM dynamics, not leader quality
2. **"People won't be honest"** → Anonymous + gamified, completion rates >85%
3. **"Can't act on findings"** → AI recommendations + priority ranking included
4. **"Employees expect changes we can't make"** → Survey measures, doesn't promise. Communication guide included.
5. **"Just another HR toy"** → Frame as CEO-level retention and P&L tool
6. **"We're too small"** → Works from 30 people. Smaller = faster impact.

## Sales Psychology Quick Reference
- **Anchoring**: Always show Premium tier first (129 CZK), then Standard (99 CZK)
- **Loss aversion**: "What does it cost when a key person leaves without warning?" > "Save money with Echo Pulse"
- **Social proof**: Czech companies only (Grammer, Vodafone, 365.bank) — never US examples
- **Reciprocity**: Free pilot creates obligation. Free benchmark report creates value before ask.
- **Scarcity**: "Máme kapacitu na 3 nové pilotní projekty tento kvartál" (capacity-based, not fake)
- **Two-option close**: Always offer A/B choice, never yes/no: "Preferujete pilot pro 1 tým nebo rovnou pro celé oddělení?"
