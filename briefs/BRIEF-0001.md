## Objective
Create 5 fresh cold email variants targeting Czech CEOs in manufacturing, IT, and logistics. Each email uses a different angle to break through inbox noise and book a 15-min call or start a free pilot.

## Target Audience
Czech CEOs (100-500 employees) in manufacturing, IT services, and logistics. They're busy, skeptical of "HR tools," care about numbers, and want proof something works before they invest time. Pain: silent turnover bleeding cash, no real visibility into team health, survey fatigue from old-school approaches.

## Key Messages
1. Silent turnover is costing you millions and you can't see it coming
2. Echo Pulse gives you real data in 48 hours — not another 6-month HR project
3. 99 CZK/person, anonymous, benchmarked against your industry — zero risk to try

## Angles (one per email variant)
1. **Cost angle** — What silent turnover actually costs (calculate for their company size)
2. **Benchmark angle** — Your competitors already know their numbers, do you?
3. **Time angle** — 48 hours from start to actionable dashboard, not 6 months
4. **Anonymity angle** — Why your people won't tell you the truth (and how to fix it)
5. **Competitor angle** — Companies in your sector are already using this

## Requirements
- Length: 80-120 words per email (short, punchy, mobile-friendly)
- Tone: Josef sales email — direct, human, no corporate fluff, slight urgency
- Must include: 99 CZK/person, 48h results, anonymous, benchmarked
- Each email: different subject line, different hook, same CTA options (15-min call or free pilot)
- Language: Czech
- No "Vazeny pane" openers — modern, first-name energy
