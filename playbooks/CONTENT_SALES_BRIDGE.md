## CopyAgent Integration Notes

When CopyAgent generates an email and needs to include an article:
1. Check prospect's `role` (CEO / HR / Team Leader) from PIPELINE_STATUS.md
2. Check `stage` (talking / proposal / negotiation / pilot)
3. Look up the best article match in this document
4. Use the framing sentence from the table above — don't invent a new one
5. Place the article link naturally after the framing sentence, not in a P.S.
6. Max 1 article per email (exception: BOFU close emails can include 2)
7. Never use "check out this article" or "read our blog" — always frame with a specific insight

### Article URL Format
```
https://behavera.com/blog/{slug}
```

Example: `https://behavera.com/blog/kolik-stoji-ticha-fluktuace`
